Route Overview (Route Overview)

This itinerary is ahigh-intensity walking route, comfortable shoes are recommended. The route logic is divided into three stages:

  1. Shinjuku Central Area: The shock of the first body swap (South Exit, Kabukicho, Nishi-Shinjuku)

  2. Taki's Commute Route: Daily life on the Chuo-Sobu Line (Sendagaya, Shinanomachi)

  3. The Final Run: The Road to Reunion from Yotsuya to Suga Shrine

1. The Awakening's Starting Point: JR Shinjuku Station South Exit

Location:JR Shinjuku Station South Exit Overpass

Movie time:20:40

Exit the South Exit and look to your right; that's the pedestrian bridge spanning Koshu Kaido."Tokyo--!!"When Mitsuha first entered Taki's body and stood at this spot, looking down at the endless stream of cars and the huge crowd, the shock of a country girl entering a big city for the first time was overwhelming. Standing here, you can almost hear that cute exclamation in your ears.

2. Melody of Zenzenzense: Yunika Vision

Location:In front of Seibu Shinjuku Station / Shinjuku Dai-Guard East

Movie Timestamp:32:02 / 1:28:18

Navigate to <Seibu Shinjuku Station>, and look up to see the iconic triple-screen shopping mall (Yunika Vision).

  • 32 minutes and 02 secondsThe stirring music of 'Zenzenzense' plays, and the camera swiftly sweeps past here.

  • 1:28:18That night, the Tiamat Comet coldly swept past above this screen, while news of the comet's approach was playing on the screen.

3. A Near Miss: Shinjuku Grand Overpass

Location:Shinjuku Police Station / In front of Sompo Japan Building

Movie Timestamp:1:37:06

Walk towards West Shinjuku and cross that famous<Shinjuku Police Station> roundabout traffic light (30-minute 'Zenzenzense' shot), continue straight and you will see this huge overpass.

This is the most poignant scene at the end of the movie. On a snowy night, adult Taki and Mitsuha passed each other on a pedestrian bridge. Both stopped, but neither looked back. Standing on the bridge, looking through the steel structure at the distantYoyogi Tower (NTT Docomo Tower), that tall tower, Makoto Shinkai's favorite, witnessed countless missed moments.

On the way to <Shinjuku Police Station> you can find the filming location of "Weathering With You", right next to <Otsuka Shokai>.

Go throughthe underpass, on the left side, you can see the scene from the movie at 1 hour, 28 minutes, and 18 seconds, where Comet Tiamat streaks across overhead.

Head intothe alley on the rightand then look back, that's where Hodaka and Hina passed through when they were being chased in "Weathering With You".

Return to the main road, continue straight ahead, and you will see aPedestrian Overpass. This is the pedestrian overpass where Taki and Mitsuha passed each other at 1 hour, 37 minutes, and 06 seconds into the movie.

From the pedestrian overpass, you can see the Yoyogi Building in the distance, which has appeared repeatedly in Makoto Shinkai's films.

Continue walking towards<Shinjuku Police Station>intersection, where a huge circular traffic light structure is erected next to it. It appeared at 30 minutes into the movieã€ŠZenzenzenseã€‹in the scene.

4. The Garden of Words Easter Egg: Shinjuku Gyoen

Location: Enter via Shinjuku Gate

Take the Marunouchi Line to Shinjuku-sanchome, and enter <Shinjuku Gyoen> . This is not only the setting for 'The Garden of Words' but also the best spot to photograph the Yoyogi Building. Makoto Shinkai's universe converges here.

5. Evolving Station: Sendagaya Station (Sendagaya)

Location: JR Sendagaya Station

Movie Timestamp: 1:39:32

Passing through Gyoen, you arrive at <Sendagaya Station> . Although the station was renovated for the Olympics, this was still Taki's daily route to school. At the end of the film, adult Taki gets off here, searching for that familiar figure on the Chuo-Sobu Line platform.

Tap your Suica card to enter and board, then go to Platform 2 and wait for theChuo-Sobu Line Local train, bound forOchanomizu, Akihabara, Chiba. This line was also Taki's daily route to school.

6. Unreachable Call: Shinanomachi Station Overpass

Location: Directly accessible from JR Shinanomachi Station exit

Movie Timestamp: 41:32 / 1:13:55

As soon as you exit Shinanomachi Station, you can see the blue overpass.

  • 41:32: Taki calls Mitsuha here, but it shows 'out of service area'.

  • 1:13:55: Mitsuha, gathering her courage, came to Tokyo and also stood here to call Taki.

Standing on the overpass, the background isGaien-higashi-dori's tree-lined avenue and the distant Yoyogi Building. Watching the passersby, you will deeply feel the loneliness of 'being in a city of millions, yet unable to reach only you'.

At 41:32 in the movie, Taki calls Mitsuha on the overpass. At 1:13:55, Mitsuha calls Taki on the overpass.

At the station exit, there is also a scene from the movie at 1:39:40, where Mitsuha passes by here while searching for Taki at the end of the film.

Gaien Higashi-dori, seen from the pedestrian bridge at 41:20 in the movie.

The Yoyogi Building at 41:25 is also depicted from this location.

The station exit is on Gaien Higashi-dori, shot towards the pedestrian bridge, which is the pedestrian bridge where Taki and Okudera-senpai had their date in the movie.

The pilgrimage of the Shinjuku chapter is a confirmation of 'memory.' From bustling to tranquil, from the missed overpass to the reunited stairs. If you complete the entire journey, you will find that it's not just about checking off locations, but about reliving the movie in the real world.
